About Enviame

TL;DR: Enviame is a priority messenger that filters important messages while respecting focus modes.

Designed for those who use "Focus Mode" but need urgent messages to bypass silencing, Enviame ensures trusted contacts can reach you while blocking distractions.

Future integrations with smartwatches and other delivery methods are planned.

Tech Stack: Rust (Axum & Tokio), PostgreSQL, Static HTML & JS
Made by: Brian
